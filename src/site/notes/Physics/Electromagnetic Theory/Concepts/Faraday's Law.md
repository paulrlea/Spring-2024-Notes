Faraday's Law relates magnetic flux to changes in electric field. Part of [[Physics/Electromagnetic Theory/Concepts/Maxwell's Laws\|Maxwell's Laws]]
$$
\oint \vec{E} \cdot d\vec{s} = -\frac{d\Phi_{b}}{dt}
$$
